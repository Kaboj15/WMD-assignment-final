What more could we say about The City of Lights, Paris. The most visited capital in the world, Paris is renowned for a multitude of things! Often referred to as the City of Light, Paris is breathtaking. It is also the home to some of the most iconic and famous buildings in France! Of course, the Eiffel Tower has to be at the top of our list! Not only is it the most visited attraction in Paris, and indeed the icon of Paris...
